🌼 Stress Buster

🧠
Feeling Stressed?
Pause. Take a deep breath. Acknowledge your feelings without judgment.
🌬️
Take a Deep Breath
Inhale slowly for 4 seconds, hold, and exhale. Repeat 3 times.
📴
Disconnect to Reconnect
Step away from screens. Go for a walk or simply sit quietly for a few minutes.
💖
Be Kind to Yourself
You're doing your best. Speak to yourself with love and patience.
